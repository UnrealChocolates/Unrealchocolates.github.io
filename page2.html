What is two minds think alike?

Two minds think alike is a term used to emphasize a coincidence, or two people reaching the same conclusion in any manner at the same time. Thinking of a response similar to each other. Simple as that! The website will respond to your choices when you pick Yes or No on the questions. It will appear green if we have the same answer and appear red if we have different answers.
